## What We Support

- Installation and first-time setup for Blocky Defendy
- Reporting bugs or crashes with details about your device/system
- Clarification of features or usage
- Feedback or suggestions for improvements

---

## What We Don’t Support

- Questions about third-party services (Blocky Defendy does not use third-party services)
- Inquiries about external accounts, advertising, or analytics (because we don’t collect or use these)

---

## Privacy Reminder

Blocky Defendy does **not** collect any personal data. Please do not send sensitive information in your support requests.
